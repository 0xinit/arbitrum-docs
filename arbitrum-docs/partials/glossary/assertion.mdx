<p>
A staked claim made by an Arbitrum <a href="/intro/glossary#validator">Validator</a> representing a claim about an Arbitrum chain's state. An <a href="/intro/glossary#assertion">Assertion</a> may, e.g., propose a new assertion, or may be a step in a <a href="/intro/glossary#challenge">Challenge</a>.
</p>
